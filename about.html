About Us

Who we are and why we exist

Navpankh Welfare Society is a non-profit organization committed to uplifting underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, and social welfare initiatives.

We believe that meaningful change begins with compassion, consistency, and community participation. Our work focuses on creating sustainable impact rather than short-term relief.

Our Vision

To build a society where every individual has access to education, dignity, and opportunities to lead a self-reliant life.

Our Mission

  • Promote quality education for children and youth
  • Support basic healthcare and wellness awareness
  • Empower communities through social initiatives
  • Encourage volunteerism and social responsibility
